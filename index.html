About US

Here is a brief description of our history:

  • 2013-2019

    Our Origins

    We started creating a physical escape room. In our team, we all love games and travels. During our trips we realised that in Rome there were not enough Escape Rooms. This is when we decided to open ours! The experience went really well. We attracted lot of customers and we had lot of fun.

  • Today

    Digitalizazion

    With Covid-19, we decided to close our Escape Rooms, and to move to a digital solution. This is when our experience with VTX started. Today, we are a start-up of young creative minds. Games are our key to understand the world and we design and develop game Experiences to bring people together wherever they are. That is why we create fun Experiences to keep teams connected. Stay motivated, stay engaged, stay remote.
